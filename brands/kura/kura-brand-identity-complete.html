01 — Brand Overview

The Brand at a Glance

KURA
Clean Surface. Clear Mind.

Brand Name

KURA — derived from the Japanese verb kurasu (to live well) and the Latin cura (care, precision). It signals a brand that takes kitchen hygiene seriously without being clinical or cold.

Hero Product Name

The KURA Board — clean, ownable, memorable. Later SKUs can follow: The KURA Board Pro, The KURA Rack, The KURA Mat.

Brand Category

Premium Kitchen Essentials — positioned above Amazon generics, below luxury cookware brands. The sweet spot where quality meets everyday use.

Tagline

"Clean Surface. Clear Mind." — speaks to both the functional benefit (hygiene) and the emotional payoff (peace of mind in the kitchen).

02 — Positioning Statement

"For home cooks who refuse to compromise on hygiene, KURA makes the only cutting board built to stay genuinely clean — not just look clean — session after session, year after year."

KURA sits between the disposable plastic boards from mass retailers and the overpriced artisan wooden boards from boutique stores. It wins on hygiene, durability, and simplicity — for people who actually cook.

03 — Target Customer

Who Buys KURA

The Home Chef

Primary Buyer · Ages 28–45

Cooks 4–6 times per week. Takes food seriously — follows recipe creators, owns good knives, keeps a clean kitchen. Tired of replacing warped boards and worrying about bacteria. Will spend more for something that actually lasts.

The Practical Parent

Secondary Buyer · Ages 30–50

Cooking for a family means raw meat, cross-contamination risks, and constant cleanup. They don't need a fancy product — they need one that they can trust is genuinely clean when it comes out of the dishwasher. KURA solves that.

The Gifter

Seasonal Buyer · All Ages

Looking for a housewarming, wedding, or birthday gift that feels premium without being cliché. A cutting board they've never seen before, packaged well, with a clear purpose. KURA earns this buyer with good branding alone.

07 — Voice & Tone

How KURA Speaks

KURA is confident but not arrogant. Direct but never cold. The brand speaks like a knowledgeable friend who happens to know a lot about kitchen hygiene — not a salesperson, not a laboratory report.

✓ KURA Does Say

  • Short sentences. Punchy. No fluff.
  • "Your old board is making you sick." — lead with the problem.
  • "One board. Zero bacteria. Zero regrets." — benefit stacking.
  • Specific numbers: 304 steel, 0.7mm thick, dishwasher safe.
  • Warm but factual — never clinical, never gimmicky.
  • "Built for cooks who take their kitchen seriously."

✗ KURA Never Says

  • "Premium quality product for discerning customers…" — generic filler.
  • Exclamation marks overload: "Amazing!!! Best board ever!!!"
  • Vague claims without substance: "revolutionary", "game-changing".
  • Over-technical jargon without payoff.
  • Anything that sounds like it was written by committee.
  • Begging for the sale: "Don't miss out! Limited time!!!"

Brand Voice in One Line

"Precise. Warm. Uncompromising."

08 — Shopify Store Guidelines

Brand Rules for the Store

Logo Usage

  • Logo is "KURA" in Bebas Neue, tracked wide (letter-spacing: 0.14em)
  • On light backgrounds: Deep Slate (#2B3445)
  • On dark backgrounds: Warm Cream (#F6EFE2)
  • Never stretch, rotate, recolor, or add a drop shadow
  • Minimum size: 80px wide for digital

Button Style

  • Primary CTA: Burnished Copper background (#C47A3A), Cream text
  • Secondary CTA: Deep Slate background, Cream text
  • Font: DM Sans 500, letter-spacing 0.1em, all caps
  • Border-radius: 4px max — KURA is sharp, not bubbly
  • No gradient buttons, no glow effects

Image Direction

  • Clean, bright lifestyle shots — modern kitchen settings
  • Show the product in actual use: chopping, rinsing, drying
  • Real kitchens > studio product shots where possible
  • Avoid: dark moody food photography (wrong category)
  • Avoid: stock photo hands on generic white backgrounds

Replo Template Mapping

  • Replace orange (#E87B35) with Copper (#C47A3A)
  • Keep the cream background — it's already on-brand
  • Keep the navy/slate — it matches Deep Slate closely
  • Replace all hair product icons with kitchen icons
  • Use Bebas Neue for all section badges and labels

Review Strategy

  • Reviewer names: use real-sounding, diverse names + city
  • Review titles: specific outcomes, not vague praise
  • Body: mention a real problem they had before, then the fix
  • Don't fake reviews — use Judge.me or Loox to collect real ones
  • Placeholder copy is for launch only — replace within 30 days

SEO & Store Name

  • Store name: KURA Kitchen or KURA Home
  • Meta title format: The KURA Board — [Benefit] | KURA Kitchen
  • Target keywords: stainless steel cutting board, non-porous cutting board, hygienic cutting board kitchen
  • Handle: /products/the-kura-board

Section 11 — FAQs (8 Questions)

Expand ↓

Q1

Will this board dull my knives?
No. The smooth 304 stainless steel surface is gentler on knife edges than most plastic and glass boards. Your blades stay sharper for longer.

Q2

Is stainless steel safe for food prep?
Absolutely. Food-grade 304 stainless steel is the same material used in professional restaurant kitchens worldwide. Non-toxic, non-porous, fully safe.

Q3

Can I put it in the dishwasher?
Yes. 100% dishwasher safe. Or rinse by hand — either way, it's clean in seconds.

Q4

Will it scratch easily?
It's scratch-resistant for normal kitchen use. Light surface marks may appear over heavy use, but they don't affect hygiene or performance.

Q5

Does it slide around on the counter?
We recommend a damp kitchen towel or silicone mat underneath for extra stability during heavy chopping.

Q6

Which size should I get?
Medium (34×23cm) for everyday prep. Large (39×28cm) for families or bigger cuts. XL (30×46cm) for serious home cooks who need full range of motion.

Q7

How do I remove odors or stains?
A quick rinse is usually enough. For stubborn residue, dish soap and a sponge removes everything. Stainless steel doesn't absorb odors.

Q8

Can I use it as a trivet?
Yes. Heat-resistant — safe for hot pots and pans directly from the stovetop or oven.
