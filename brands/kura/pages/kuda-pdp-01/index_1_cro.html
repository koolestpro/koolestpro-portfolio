FAQS

Will this board dull my knives?+
No. The smooth 304 stainless steel surface is gentler on knife edges than most plastic and glass boards. Your blades stay sharper for longer.
Is stainless steel safe for food prep?+
Absolutely. Food-grade 304 stainless steel is the same material used in professional restaurant kitchens worldwide. Non-toxic, non-porous, fully safe.
Can I put it in the dishwasher?+
Yes. 100% dishwasher safe. Or rinse by hand — either way, it's clean in seconds.
Will it scratch easily?+
It's scratch-resistant for normal kitchen use. Light surface marks may appear over time with heavy use, but they won't affect hygiene or performance.
Does it slide around on the counter?+
We recommend a damp kitchen towel or silicone mat underneath for extra stability during heavy chopping sessions.
Which size should I get?+
Small (30×23cm) — great for compact kitchens or light prep work.
Medium (34×23cm) — ideal for everyday prep, the most popular size.
Large (39×28cm) — best for families, meal prep, and bigger cuts of meat.
How do I remove odors or stains?+
A quick rinse is usually enough. For stubborn residue, dish soap and a sponge removes everything. Stainless steel doesn't absorb odors.
Can I use it as a trivet?+
Yes. Heat-resistant and safe for hot pots and pans directly from the stovetop or oven.
