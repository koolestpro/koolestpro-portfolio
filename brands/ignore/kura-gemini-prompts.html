Pro Tips

Getting the Best Results

Gemini ImageFX Workflow Tips

  • Run 4 variations per prompt — Gemini generates multiple outputs. The best result is rarely the first one.
  • Click to select all the prompt text (each box is selectable by clicking), then copy with Ctrl/Cmd+C.
  • Add the Universal Modifier at the end of every prompt for brand consistency across your asset library.
  • Be specific about lighting: Specify "warm window light from left" or "diffused overhead studio light" — lighting direction dramatically changes the mood.
  • Edit iteratively: After generating, use Gemini's inpainting to edit specific areas (e.g., "make the board larger", "change the background to cream"). This is faster than re-prompting from scratch.
  • For logo concepts: Use the Logo prompts with Gemini's image generation, then recreate the best concept as a clean SVG using the kura-icon.svg file as your base. AI-generated logos are inspiration, not final production files.
  • For product shots: If you get the composition right but the colors are off, use a follow-up prompt: "Adjust the color balance — warmer cream tones in the background, cooler steel tones on the board surface."
  • Batch your sessions: Generate all 6 product photography prompts in one sitting so your entire gallery has consistent lighting and tone.
