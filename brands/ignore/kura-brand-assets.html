05 — Logo Usage Rules

Do's & Don'ts

✓ Always Do
  • Use "KURA" in Bebas Neue with letter-spacing 0.14em for the wordmark
  • On light backgrounds: Deep Slate (#2B3445) fill
  • On dark backgrounds: Warm Cream (#F6EFE2) fill
  • Maintain minimum 24px width for the icon mark
  • Keep the copper accent rule as part of the K-mark icon
  • Use the provided SVG source — scale from vector, never upscale raster
  • Give the logo clear space equal to half its width on all sides
✗ Never Do
  • Don't stretch, rotate, or skew the logo in any direction
  • Don't recolor the logo in any color outside the brand palette
  • Don't add drop shadows, glows, gradients, or outlines
  • Don't place the logo on a busy photograph without sufficient contrast
  • Don't use the icon mark smaller than 24px — use wordmark only instead
  • Don't use any other font for the KURA wordmark
  • Don't place the copper K-mark on the copper background
